Introduction

Montreal Logo
Montreal, officially Montréal (French)is the most populous municipality in Quebec and the 2nd-most populous in Canada. Originally called Ville-Marie, or "City of Mary". It is believed to be named after Mount Royal, though there is some debate about this. The city occupies most of the Island of Montreal, as well as many smaller peripheral islands, most significantly Île Bizard. The city has a distinct four-season continental climate, with warm to hot summers and cold snowy winters.
